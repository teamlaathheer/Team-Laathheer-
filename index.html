Deep Laath

Deep Laath is a passionate artist, singer, writer, and poet from Bassai, Mahendergarh. His debut song as singer and lyricist Rao Shabh te Yaari released in January 2025.

He has contributed to many Haryanvi hits like: Let Me Love You, Dil Me Bhola, 2 Ghode, Gulaab, Taare, Rang, SYL, Agneepath, Kisan, Desi Man and many more.

Birthday: 8 May

Instagram @p_deep_laath

Mohit Heer

Mohit Heer, also from Bassai, is a soulful lyricist and poet. His lyrics reflect deep emotion and rural roots. He is also the lyricist and co-singer of Rao Shabh te Yaari.

Popular songs include: Agniveer, Kai Din, December, Yaar Bamb, Kismat Te Rao Sahab, Kedarnath, Riyasat etc.

Birthday: 9 February

Instagram @mohit__heer
