Deep Laath

Deep Laath is a singer, writer, and shayar from Bassai village, Mahendergarh. He has been active in this field for years and released his debut song as an artist in January 2025 titled "Rao Shabh Te Yaari". Known for powerful lyrics and soulful delivery, he has written and performed many songs like Let Me Love You, Dil Me Bhola, SYL, Desi Man, Taare, Gulab, Rang, Riyasat, Babu Mere, Purusharth, and more.

Birthday: 8 May

Instagram Spotify

Mohit Heer

Mohit Heer, also from Bassai (Mahendergarh), is an exceptional writer and poet. His lyrical talent shines through songs like Agniveer, Yaar Bamb, Kai Din, December, Kismat Te, and many more. Apart from songwriting, he is known for his beautiful shayaris shared on his Instagram page.

Birthday: 9 February

Instagram Spotify

Team Laathheer

Team Laathheer is a collaboration of passionate young artists aiming to bring authentic Haryanvi music and poetry to the world. Main team members include Yogesh and Navdeep, working behind the scenes with full dedication.
