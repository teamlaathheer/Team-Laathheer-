Deep Laath

Deep Laath is a growing artist, singer, writer, and shayar from Bassai village in Mahendragarh district. Active in the field for many years, his debut as a singer was in January 2025 with the song "Rao Shabh Te Yaari". He has written and worked on many tracks like Let Me Love You, Dil Me Bhola, 2 Ghode, SYL, Kisan, Agneepath, Desi Man, Taare, Gulab, Kai Din, Rang, Riyasat, Babu Mere, Purusharth, Kedarnath, and Kismat Te Rao Sahab.

Birthday: 8 May

Mohit Heer

Mohit Heer, also from Bassai village in Mahendragarh, is a talented writer and poet. Known for his meaningful lyrics and thoughtful lines, he has worked on songs like Agniveer, Kai Din, December, Yaar Bamb, and many more. He is also known for his deep shayari style and presence on social media.

Birthday: 9 February
