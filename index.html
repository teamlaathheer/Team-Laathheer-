👤 Know the Artists

🎤 Deep Laath

Deep Laath is a passionate singer, writer, and shayar from Bassai, Mahendergarh. He has been working in the industry for years, and released his debut song "Rao Shabh Te Yaari" in January 2025.

Other notable songs include: Let Me Love You, Dil Me Bhola, 2 Ghode, SYL, Kisan, Agneepath, Desi Man, Taare, Gulab, Gulaab, Kai Din, Rang, Riyasat, Babu Mere, Purusharth, Kedarnath, Kismat Te Rao Sahab, and many more.

Birthday: 8 May

Instagram: @p_deep_laath

Spotify: Deep Laath on Spotify

✍️ Mohit Heer

Mohit Heer is a talented lyricist and poet, also from Bassai, Mahendergarh. His debut as a singer and lyricist came with the song "Rao Shabh Te Yaari".

He has written songs like Agniveer, Yaar Bamb, Kai Din, December, Kismat Te, and more. His writing brings emotional depth and rural soul to modern Haryanvi music.

Birthday: 9 February

Instagram: @mohit__heer
